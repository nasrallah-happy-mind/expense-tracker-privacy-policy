1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ExpenseTracker (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles user information. By using the ExpenseTracker application, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

ExpenseTracker does not collect, store, or share any personal information on external servers.

All data you enter in the app—such as expenses, categories, notes, or budgets—is stored locally on your device only.

We do not collect:

  • Personal identity data (such as your name, email address, or phone number)
  • Bank account or payment card information
  • Location data (GPS or network-based)
  • Contacts, photos, or other media from your device

3. How We Use Information

Because all information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us:

  • We do not process or analyze your data on any server.
  • We do not share your data with any third parties.
  • Your information remains fully under your control on your own device.

4. Third-Party Services

ExpenseTracker does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services inside the app.

However, the app may rely on services provided by Google Play (for example, for app updates, distribution, or crash reporting). These services are governed by their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

5. Data Security

Since your data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to us, the primary responsibility for protecting this data lies with you as the device owner.

We recommend that you:

  • Use a secure screen lock (PIN, password, fingerprint, etc.).
  • Regularly back up your device if needed.
  • Keep your operating system and apps updated.

6. Children’s Privacy

ExpenseTracker is not directed specifically at children. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Continued use of the application after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

لا يقوم تطبيق ExpenseTracker بجمع أو تخزين أو مشاركة أي بيانات شخصية على خوادم خارجية.

جميع البيانات التي يقوم المستخدم بإدخالها داخل التطبيق — مثل المصروفات، الفئات، الملاحظات أو الميزانية — يتم تخزينها محليًا على جهاز المستخدم فقط.

ولا نقوم بجمع ما يلي:

  • بيانات الهوية الشخصية (مثل الاسم أو البريد الإلكتروني أو رقم الهاتف)
  • بيانات الحسابات البنكية أو بطاقات الدفع
  • بيانات الموقع الجغرافي
  • جهات الاتصال أو الصور أو أي وسائط من جهازك

٣. كيفية استخدام البيانات

  • لا نقوم بمشاركة بياناتك مع أي طرف ثالث.
